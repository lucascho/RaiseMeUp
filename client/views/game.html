Welcome to RaiseMeUp!

Here are some instructions for you to get started:

Objective:
Place cubes onto the grid to build pathways that helps the "round man" escape from the rising water.

Controls:
To place a cube: double click on the grid
To rotate views: click and drag the mouse
To trigger AI for the "round man": press "=" on keyboard
To control "round man" movement yourself: keyboard "W", "A", "S", "D"

*Note: Cubes are limited resources! Make sure you lead the "round man" to the coins to build higher!
You can either play single player mode or play with others. You just need to share a room number.
